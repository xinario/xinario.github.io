Biography

I am currently working for Circle focusing on developing advanced techniques for cardiac image analysis. Before that, I have spent 7 years in academia, first as a PhD student in general image processing and then as a postdoctoral fellow in medical image analysis.

Although my passion for computer vision comes from T-800, the terminator, I hope my work will benefit human being and make the world a better place.
